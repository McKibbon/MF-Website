About Us

The Marigold Foundation is a registered Canadian charity. Over the past 30 years, we have earned a strong reputation for being hands-on: we use a business-like approach and, as an operating foundation, take a very active role in areas that are meaningful to us.

Our current programs are committed to the development of meaningful treatments for orphan diseases, particularly myotonic muscular dystrophy. We are passionate in this and driven to get results.

We welcome partnerships in areas of mutual interest. Please contact us if you would like more information about us or our current programs.
